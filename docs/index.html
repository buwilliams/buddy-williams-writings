2026
Structural Convergence Thesis Essay

I've been troubled by the orthogonality thesis: the idea that AI goals and intelligence are fully independent. I don't think it holds up once you factor in finite minds, relational pressure, and reflective reach. This essay argues that persistent minds converge on a relational Goldilocks zone, and that AI safety depends on maturation, not just control.

Metaprogramming Framework To Explore Personhood → lumen-mind Essay + Project

Personhood has fascinated me for years. The hard problem of consciousness always felt like a framing error. My theory: personhood is substrate-independent metaprogramming: information looking at itself. The difference between human and AI minds is maturity, not kind. Once I had the theory, I had to build it. Lumen-mind is a working mind architecture for AI agents.

AI Is a Phase Change Essay

Everyone is asking "how intelligent is it?" but that's the wrong question. The right questions are orthogonal: what is it, what can it do, and what does it mean for how we live? I wrote this to untangle the three questions people keep conflating, and to name the map-territory trap that lets people hide from what's already happening.

Technohumanism → euda Essay + Project

If Technohumanism is right, aligned abundance is where we're headed. Personal intelligence should serve human flourishing, not just productivity. Euda is an experiment in what that looks like.

Can LLMs Create Knowledge? → thought-experiment-generator Essay + Project

A response to Brett Hall on Deutsch's epistemology. The question isn't whether LLMs can scale their way to new knowledge. It's whether an architecture built above the model can navigate the space where knowledge actually lives. The search space is ~10^5000. Pure iteration is intractable. But collapsing the space through layered filters is tractable. I built the thought-experiment-generator to test the idea.

Objective Morality Research

Technohumanism + The Outcome Economy → harmonic Essays + Project

Technology transforms scarcity into abundance. Knowledge workers become intelligence workers. Outcome-based models replace time-based ones. These weren't just ideas. They pointed at something to build. HarmonicWork.ai is an AI-human work orchestration platform that eliminated planning meetings by routing work automatically to the optimal human or AI worker. Concept to production in 8 weeks. Watch the product video or see a live client session.

Cyclic Rationality Essay

I've always believed imagination should precede verification: qualitative dreaming is what opens up the space of possibilities before you measure anything. This is the methodology I use. Qual without Quan produces delusional dreamers; Quan without Qual produces smart idiots. The cycle between them is where discovery lives.

AI Economics: The Time Traveler Essay

What happens when productivity becomes nearly infinite? The interesting answer: meaning becomes the scarce resource. I wrote this as a thought experiment, AI as time travel, to work out what abundance actually costs us and what it gives us back.

Economics in the Intelligence Age Essay

A first-principles layered model: scarcity → prices → externalities → power → AI. I wanted to understand where AI fits in the economic story, not just what it disrupts. This is the framework I use to think about it.

Why Hypotheticals Matter Essay

Thought experiments feel annoying until you realize they're the only tool that reveals what's hidden by everyday noise. This is my defense of the hypothetical, and a guide to how I use them.

The Utility of Truth Essay

Evolution doesn't optimize for truth. It optimizes for survival. Understanding why minds resist change is the first step to planting ideas that might actually grow. This is my model for how to do that.
