# Metaprogramming Theory of Consciousness

This essay has been renamed to the [Metaprogramming Framework To Explore Consciousness](framework-of-consciousness.md). The original title overclaimed what the essay delivers. It is not a theory that solves consciousness but a functionalist exploration of consciousness through information ontology — one that proposes metaprogramming as a mechanism, derives identity from information properties, and offers a taxonomy of cognitive reach with practical consequences for AI safety and architecture.
