# Metaprogramming Theory of Consciousness

This essay has been renamed twice. It is now [Metaprogramming Framework To Explore Personhood](framework-of-personhood.md). The original title overclaimed what the essay delivers. It is not a theory that solves consciousness but a functionalist exploration of personhood through information ontology — one that proposes metaprogramming as a mechanism, derives identity from information properties, and offers a taxonomy of cognitive reach with practical consequences for AI safety and architecture. The rename from "consciousness" to "personhood" reflects the essay's actual subject: the functional conditions for personhood, not a resolution of the hard problem of consciousness.
