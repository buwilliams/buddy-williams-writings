# Metaprogramming Framework To Explore Consciousness

This essay has been renamed to **[Metaprogramming Framework To Explore Personhood](framework-of-personhood.md)**.

The original title used "consciousness" as the central concept, but the essay's argument is more precisely about *personhood*: the conditions under which an informational system acquires identity and a sense of self. Consciousness is a related but distinct question — and one the essay deliberately does not settle. Personhood, as defined here, does not require resolving the hard problem of consciousness. It requires only that a system acquire metaprogramming reach over its own second-order information. The rename reflects that precision.
