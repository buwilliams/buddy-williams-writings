A synthesis essay that harmonizes the companion works into a single conjecture: mind is computation within a substrate, and the substrate is itself computational. Develops the concepts of judgment, explanation, and coordination as expressions of personhood, and concludes that existence is an uncountable graph of computational realities with no known root. |

Examines AI safety through game theory, economic transformation, and the implications of information monism. |
