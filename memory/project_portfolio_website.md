Buddy wants to build a portfolio website with these requirements:
- Resume in website form AND downloadable form
- Stays updated (ideally auto-syncs from this GitHub repo of writings)
- Easy for AI to repurpose for specific job applications (structured resume data as JSON/YAML)
- Positions him as a "technical creative" — someone who arrives at edges before they're edges and builds the first version
- Not a "hire me" site — a "find me" site for the rare right people

Target roles: Chief AI Officer, VP AI Strategy, Head of Innovation, AI Research Lead, Founding AI Advisor — at AI-native startups, frontier labs, or large innovation functions.

**Why:** Most jobs are designed for cogs. Buddy is a pioneer. He needs to find the rare organizations where his edge-detection and framework-building style is valued, not tolerated.

**How to apply:** When helping with this project, emphasize identity-first framing (not job titles), lead with thinking quality (essays as proof of mind), and treat the resume as structured data with a rendered layer on top.
