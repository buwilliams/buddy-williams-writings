## The Ideas

- **Mathematics is incomplete** — Kurt Gödel

- **All math is not provable** — Alan Turing

- **Probability and statistical methods miss priors; there is no fair coin** — Aubrey Clayton

- **Science is mainly focused on reduction methods while nature shows things as more than the sum of their parts** — Melanie Mitchell

- **People primarily pursue ideology and tribalism, not truth** — Jonathan Haidt

- **All human knowledge is a model, always a reduction of reality** — Plato, Aristotle, John Locke

- **We may live in a simulation** — Nick Bostrom

- **Reality may be better understood as a computation rather than through mathematics (likely unprovable)** — Stephen Wolfram
