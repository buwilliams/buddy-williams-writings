## The Core Insight

Even if we erased every billionaire tomorrow, the hierarchy would rebuild itself.

**The problem isn't the billionaires — it's human nature.**

Status-seeking is a feature of tribal coordination, not a bug.

---

## The Goal

The goal isn't to flatten the pyramid.

It's to **shorten it**: wider base, shorter peak, shared prosperity.
